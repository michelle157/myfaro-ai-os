---

## Common objection patterns
| Objection | Reframe |
|-----------|---------|
| "We already have a CRM / Brio / Brokercloud." | myFaro is the advisory, compliance and reporting workflow layer across suppliers — it coexists with the CRM/BOAR. |
| "We use supplier portals already." | Portals remain execution channels; myFaro consolidates across suppliers into one structured workflow. |
| "Is this compliant / FSMA-proof?" | myFaro supports and structures an audit-ready process; it does not replace expert/legal validation. Avoid guarantees. |
| "Too expensive / not now." | Anchor on manual-work reduction and structure; propose a scoped first milestone or POC. |
| "We'll build this ourselves." | Position the horizontal, multi-supplier operating layer and time-to-value vs internal build. |

### Guardrails
- Fair language; no invented competitor features.
- No compliance guarantees.
- Always end on a next step.
