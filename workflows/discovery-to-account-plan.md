# Workflow — Discovery to Account Plan

## Purpose
Convert discovery findings into a structured account plan with the strongest use case, demo angle and next steps.

## Owner agent
`agents/sales-playbook-agent.md`

## Trigger
Discovery call completed and notes captured.

## Inputs
- Discovery notes or `templates/discovery-summary.md`.
- Account and segment context from `memory/03_ICP_AND_GTM_STRATEGY.md` and `08_PARTNERSHIPS_AND_KEY_ACCOUNTS.md`.

## Steps
1. Confirm ICP, current systems and operating model.
2. Identify main pains and map them to myFaro use cases (portfolio overview, compliance workflow, pension/80%-rule, reporting, group standardization).
3. Select the strongest demo angle and opening narrative.
4. Map stakeholders and decision process.
5. Define first milestone, POC scope (if relevant) and next steps with owners.
6. Populate `templates/account-plan.md`.

## Output
- Completed account plan (`templates/account-plan.md`).
- Recommended demo plan (`templates/demo-script.md`).

## Guardrails
- Identify ICP and use case before recommending an angle.
- No invented pricing, discounts or roadmap.
- Keep scope focused; avoid scope creep.
