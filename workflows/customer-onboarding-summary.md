# Workflow — Customer Onboarding Summary

## Purpose
Turn an onboarding kickoff or status conversation into a structured onboarding summary with a clear first milestone and sequenced next steps.

## Owner agent
`agents/customer-success-agent.md`

## Trigger
A new customer starts onboarding, or an onboarding checkpoint occurs.

## Inputs
- Kickoff notes or transcript.
- Customer context: segment, systems, users, goals.

## Steps
1. Capture customer goals, users and current systems.
2. Define the first milestone and success criteria.
3. Sequence onboarding steps with owners and dates.
4. Identify data-quality and adoption risks.
5. Note expansion signals to revisit after value is proven.

## Output
- Onboarding summary with first milestone and next steps.
- Adoption/data-quality risk list.

## Guardrails
- Always define the first milestone; avoid scope creep.
- Prioritize adoption and data quality.
- Only flag expansion after value is demonstrated.
- Keep commitments realistic; no invented roadmap.
