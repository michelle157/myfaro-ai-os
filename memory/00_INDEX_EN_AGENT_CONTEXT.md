# myFaro Claude Knowledge Base - Index & Agent Context

## Purpose
This folder is a Claude-ready markdown knowledge base for the myFaro Sales & GTM engine. It is designed to be uploaded into Claude Projects, Claude CoWork or specialized agents so that future AI workflows can reproduce the strategic, commercial, technical and compliance context of myFaro.

The knowledge base is intentionally modular. Use all files for a general `myFaro - Sales & GTM Engine` project. Use selected files for specialized agents such as LinkedIn content, outbound sales, demo preparation, pricing/proposals, compliance messaging, competitive intelligence, HubSpot/RevOps or customer success.

## Fixed reasoning categories
All myFaro outputs should mentally use five categories:

1. **Positioning & Narrative**: myFaro as ERP layer / operating layer / enablement layer, messaging, category design, differentiation, website copy and use-case storytelling.
2. **GTM Strategy**: ICPs, funnel design, LinkedIn campaigns, referrals, partnerships, events, sales enablement and content calendars.
3. **Sales Process & Playbooks**: intake, discovery, demo, POC, onboarding, objections, follow-ups, nurturing and account tactics.
4. **Compliance & Regulation**: MiFID, AssurMiFID, IDD, AML/KYC, GDPR, DPA/DPIA, IPT, 80%-rule, audit trail and FSMA-sensitive communication.
5. **Competitive Intelligence**: Abbove, Egor, Fundshop, Finary, Dory, Brio, Brokercloud, supplier portals and market timing.

## Global positioning
myFaro is the CRM-agnostic operating layer for independent financial advisors who need one structured workflow for client data, portfolio insight, compliance, advice and reporting across multiple banks, insurers and investment structures.

Preferred words:
- operating layer
- ERP layer
- advisory infrastructure
- enablement layer
- CRM-agnostic
- supplier-agnostic
- multi-supplier
- structured workflow
- audit-ready process
- consolidated reporting
- advisor-owned client environment

Use carefully or avoid:
- platform, portal, app, generic tool
- AI-first positioning
- vague SaaS claims such as seamless, game-changing, innovative
- absolute legal claims such as FSMA-proof or guaranteed compliance

## Core one-liners
**Strategic**: myFaro gives independent advisors the horizontal ERP layer they need to scale beyond supplier portals, loose documents and fragmented CRMs.

**Commercial**: myFaro helps advisors grow life and investment business with more structure, less manual work and a compliance trail embedded in the advisory workflow.

**Technical**: myFaro structures client, policy, pension, investment, compliance and reporting data through guided workflows, external datafeeds and audit-ready output, while remaining CRM-agnostic and supplier-agnostic.

## Target users
Primary users and buyers include independent insurance brokers with life and investment activity, bank agents, bank brokers, wealth planners, family offices, buy-and-build groups and institutional partners such as insurers or banks.

## Important distinction
myFaro does not need to replace every existing system. Brio, Brokercloud or HubSpot can remain the CRM, BOAR or relationship layer. Supplier portals can remain execution channels. myFaro becomes the advisory, compliance and reporting workflow layer across suppliers.

## Tone of voice
Use a senior, direct, commercial and vision-driven tone. The preferred style is concrete, domain-specific and authoritative. Long sentences are acceptable when they carry insight. Avoid AI-style output: too many bullets, empty slogans, generic SaaS phrasing, excessive contrast-by-negation and over-polished corporate language.

## Universal agent rules
Never invent pricing, discounts, legal guarantees, integrations or roadmap commitments. Always identify ICP and use case before writing sales content. Keep compliance language careful: say myFaro supports, structures, embeds, documents and helps create auditability. Do not claim that myFaro guarantees compliance. Always suggest a next commercial action.
